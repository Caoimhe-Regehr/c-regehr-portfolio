This design was an assignment in one of our illustration classes. We were taked with creating a design for a skateboard deck. It was given to us as an open canvas, and I opted to base my design off Batman the Animated Series. This design is meant to display a cityscape of Gotham, as seen in the opening of the show. To accomplish this, I used a variety of rectangular shapes, with the taller ones in the back and the shorter ones in the front. To further sell the depth, I made the buildings further back darker than the buildings in the front. I then added some watercolour texturing to the image to keep the individual layers from feeling flat. I added some basic windows to the buildings, and then I quickly painted the sky with some digital watercolour brushes in red and black, to contrast the cooler blue tones present in the buildings
